Who am I?

I’m a graduate student at the Savannah College of Art and Design, pursuing a Master of Arts in Design Management, where I explore the intersection of design, business strategy, and innovation. With a background in Management Information Systems, I bring an analytical mindset to creative problem solving. My work is rooted in a belief that design is great when it is focused on making people's lives better. I’m especially interested in projects that connect sustainability, education, and design strategy. I enjoy collaborating across disciplines to turn insights into impactful, actionable ideas.
